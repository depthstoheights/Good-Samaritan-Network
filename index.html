About The Good Samaritan Network

This interactive experience brings the parable of the Good Samaritan into modern contexts. Every choice you make affects a global network of kindness that spans cultures and situations.

Through scenarios involving online interactions, community crises, and global challenges, discover what it means to be a neighbor in today's interconnected world.

Need Help?

This app provides spiritual encouragement and education, not medical advice.

If you're in crisis (U.S.):
Call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Remember: Every act of kindness, no matter how small, ripples through the network and touches lives you may never see.
